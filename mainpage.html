Vellichor

Our workshop aims to make people who like writing be more creative and express their talent through writing stories, novels, etc. The workshop will have a separate place where person’s who like writing could make their stories. It would also have a place where people can read those stories and appreciate the talent of the underrated writers. Our workshop will have the section for the stories of the attendees and will also have some stories from the popular authors.
